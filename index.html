About Maximized Comics

Colin Maxwell is person behind Maximized. Colin has worked in photography and design for over 20 years, and specialises in video editing and animation. He has worked in education for over 15 years, working both in the classroom and online. Some of his current education work involves providing workshops for Adobe Generation Pro, a series of courses aimed at improving the skills and knowledge of educators who would like to bring digital technology to their classrooms.

In 2013 he began producing comic books along with illustrator Michael Philp. Their first comic book 'King Robert the Bruce and the Wars of Independence' was published in 2014, coinciding with the 700th anniversary of the Battle of Bannockburn.

Colin was runner up in the Dundee Comics Prize 2016, annual awards for short story writing.
